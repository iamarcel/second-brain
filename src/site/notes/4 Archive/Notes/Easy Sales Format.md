tags:: [[3 Resources/Marketing\|Marketing]] 

- Make a bold claim 
- Tell a story about your offer 
- Explain the problem 
- Explain your solution 
- Prove your solution 
- Give a guarantee 
- Call to action

9/10 times, this is all you need to convert.
